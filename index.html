HOW TO PLAY (Two-Player Game): Players alternate placing dots on a 2x2 board made up of 2x2 boxes, attempting to score by placing the last dot in each box. A player goes an extra turn whenever they score. Game ends when a player wins by reaching a match score higher than 2 or when all dots are placed.
