HOW TO PLAY (Two Player Game): Players alternate placing dots on a 5x5 board of 2x2 boxes, attempting to score by placing the last dot in each 2x2 box. A player goes an extra turn whenever they score. Game ends and winner is determined when a player reaches a match score of 13.
